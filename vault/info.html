How Vault Works

Discover how our app helps you track, share, and enjoy concerts with friends through our intuitive features.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with just a few taps using your email or social media accounts. Customize your profile with your music preferences and follow your favorite artists. Your secure account saves all your concert memories and ticket purchases in one place.

2

Discover Music You Love

Browse upcoming concerts and featured artists in your area. Our curated selection helps you find shows that match your music taste, with personalized recommendations based on your listening history and favorite artists.

3

Find Nearby Shows

Use our interactive map to discover concerts happening around you. Filter by genre, date, and price range to find the perfect show. Our location-based search helps you plan your concert calendar with venue details and directions built right in.

4

Personal Concert Dashboard

Track your concert attendance history and upcoming shows all in one place. View statistics about your music preferences, most visited venues, and favorite artists. Your dashboard provides a visual journey of your concert experiences over time.

5

Share Your Experiences

Post photos, videos, and reviews from concerts you've attended. See what shows your friends are attending and what they thought about recent performances. Our social feed keeps you connected to the live music community around you.

6

Stay Updated with Activities

Receive notifications about new concerts from your favorite artists, price drops, and friend activities. Our app keeps you in the loop about important concert information including venue changes, cancellations, and weather alerts.

7

Plan Together

Use our built-in chat feature to coordinate concert plans with friends. Create group conversations for specific shows, share ticket information, and plan meet-up locations. Make concert-going a truly social experience with real-time communication.
